    About this application

    Asian carp are an invasive species that have to potential to outcompete native fish for resources. They were originally introduced to the United States in the 1970's as a cleaning mechanism in aquaculture but escaped and spread into the Mississippi River. Silver carp specifically pose a danger to boaters because they jump out of the water when startled by passing motors.
